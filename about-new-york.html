☆ About New York ☆

New York City (to distinguish it from the state of New York) is the most populous city in the United States and is located at the southern tip of New York State, on one of the largest natural harbors in the world. The city is made up of five boroughs: Manhattan, The Bronx, Brooklyn, Queens, and Staten Island.

It is a global center of culture, finance, media, research, healthcare, fashion, and technology.

The city’s origins date back to 1624 when Dutch settlers founded it as a trading post under the name “New Amsterdam”. It was renamed New York when the English took control in 1664.

Today, New York City is considered one of the most important financial and cultural capitals in the world.
